Welcome to my blog!

I'm François Le Nôtre - a software test analyst currently based in Lyon area, France.

I have been involved in testing for over six years now, testing a wide range of interfaces in different sectors and technical environments. Over the last years I specialized in mobile websites, native applications, data and web services manual testing. I also have a passion for exploratory testing.

I really enjoy sharing knowledge, which is why I contributed to software testing conferences previously, such as the Eurostar one; I regularly attend IT Meetup events, online webinars, read books and blogs about testing, constantly seeking for learning and inspiration.

Starting to write articles about my own experience is just another way of sharing my own knowledge; I would love to hear any feedback you have or to exchange with you on any topic or questions you have. Feel free to contact me via the links in the footer: Twitter, email, Viadeo and LinkedIn, where you can also view my achievements.
